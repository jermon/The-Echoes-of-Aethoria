"I can teach you much, and I sense you have much to teach as well. The artifact chose well when it chose you." Over the following days, your visitor remains at the glade, sharing knowledge accumulated over centuries. They explain the true nature of the artifact, the history of the ancient civilization that created it, and the role that bearers of the artifact have traditionally played in the world. They warn you of dangers you haven't considered, and they help you understand the full scope of what your new power means. By the time they depart, you have a powerful ally and a vast wealth of knowledge. The visitor leaves you with a gift: a small crystal that will allow them to find you if great need arises. You've found not just knowledge, but a mentor and friend.
