--- layout: game title: Creature's History choices: - text: Learn through shared memory next_scene: ancestral_memory - text: Continue as travel companion next_scene: travel_companion - text: Seek answers elsewhere next_scene: journey_onward --- Through your connection with the creature, you begin to understand its history. Images and emotions flow between you—a legacy of centuries, of watching the world change, of standing alone as others of its kind vanished. The creature came from a time when magic flowed openly through the world and beings like it roamed freely. As humans expanded and civilization grew, magic retreated, and the creature's kind faded into legend. This one, the last of its line, stayed hidden in the forest, protecting the place where the artifact was kept. It was a guardian, though it had begun to lose faith that anyone would ever come to appreciate or need its service. Your friendship represents a reconnection with a purpose it thought lost. This knowledge deepens your respect for the creature and strengthens your bond.
