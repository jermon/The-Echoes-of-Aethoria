Every detail is significant—the proportions follow mathematical precision, the carvings tell stories in symbolic language, the runes form patterns that suggest both function and purpose. The tower is a work of genius, designed not just for physical strength but for magical resonance. You begin to understand that the structure itself is a magical artifact as much as the crystalline object you carry. The tower and the artifact are connected somehow, designed to work in harmony with each other. This realization suggests that your presence here is not accidental. The tower was waiting for someone with the artifact, and that someone might be you. The question is whether you're ready to find out what that means.
