The Fallen Troll

The forest troll lies motionless before you, its massive frame sprawled across the undergrowth. As you catch your breath, you realize this is an opportunity to claim some of its possessions—powerful artifacts that may aid you in your quest.

Before you lies an assortment of items that fell from the troll's grasp. These treasures, forged or gathered through ages of dwelling in this mystical forest, now await your decision. Drag any items you wish to keep into your inventory compartments.
